Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new collaborative session?

Click on "New Session" in the dashboard and share the generated link with your team members. Each session has a unique ID that allows your team to join and collaborate in real-time.

Why is my code not updating in real-time?

Ensure you have a stable internet connection and that WebSockets are not blocked by your network. Try refreshing the page or checking if your browser supports WebSockets.

How does cursor tracking work with multiple users?

Our collaborative editor uses color-coded cursors to identify different users. Each user gets assigned a unique color, and user names appear next to their cursors for easy identification.

Which programming languages are supported?

We support JavaScript, Python, Java, C++, Ruby, PHP, Go, TypeScript, C#, Swift, Kotlin, and Rust. Each language includes syntax highlighting and auto-completion.
